Privacy Policy for FamilyShopper
Last updated: 27.04.2026
FamilyShopper is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected and used.
1. Information Collection
   We do not require user registration or accounts.
   Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
   Technical Identifiers: The app generates a unique anonymous ID (UUID) and uses a "Group Key" provided by the user to synchronize lists. These identifiers are not linked to your real identity.
2. Data Storage and Third-Party Services
   We use Firebase Realtime Database (a service by Google) to store and synchronize your shopping lists in real-time when a Group Key is provided.
   Data is stored securely on Firebase servers.
   When the Group Key is empty, the app operates locally, and no data is sent to the network.
3. Data Usage
   The collected anonymous data is used solely to:
   Synchronize lists between users with the same Group Key.
   Ensure the technical functionality of the application.
4. User Control
   Users have full control over their data:
   You can change or delete your Group Key at any time.
   You can delete your lists within the app.
5. Children's Privacy
   Our app is safe for all users and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
6. Contact Us
   If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the developer profile in AppGallery.
